Lights Out!

On this page you'll find 4 different variations of the game "Lights Out".
The standard games use the "normal" expected rules:

  • Click a square to swap its state.
  • The directly adjacent squares (Top, Bottom, Left & Right) will also change their states.

    • The modified games use the following rules:

  • Click a square to swap its state.
  • The directly adjacent and diagonally adjacent squares will also change their states.

    • Both versions come with a 3x3 and a 5x5 board size as well as for fun, a "Bogo solve" button. Bogo solve is named after the infamous "Bogosort" which is a joke sorting algorithm that works by randomly shuffling a list until it is sorted. As such, you can probably guess that this button also randomly clicks elements until the game is solved. It's... questionably useful.
    Additionally, each game has a timer that starts upon changing the first square and stops when the game is solved.
